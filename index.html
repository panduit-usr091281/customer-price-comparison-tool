Model Assumptions

  • Unit rates are based on the provided Panduit planning sheets and normalized for single-site comparisons.
  • Labor day assumes 8 hours. Distance influences both material quantity and installation effort.
  • Class 2 is strongest for low-power scenarios and may require parallel runs at higher loads.
  • Class 4 includes head-end and receiver hardware allocation plus fiber and CL4 pathway work.
